What I Bring

Strategy, craft, and the rigor to connect them.

Design Leadership

I build design cultures. Rituals, standards, ways of working that elevate teams, not just individuals. I lead from alongside my team.

Product Strategy

I ground my decisions in business goals and the user goals, and influence roadmap direction to create beautiful experience that people will use.

Research and Data

I build the case for design with evidence. Qualitative research, quantitative data, A/B tests, usability sessions. I use all of it to prioritize, defend, and improve.

AI Workflows

Figma Make, Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Vercel. I integrate AI to accelerate delivery and sharpen iteration without sacrificing craft.
